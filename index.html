Background

Full CV in PDF (last update: September 2, 2020)

  • 2019-present

    Flowers lab, Inria, Bordeaux

    Permanent researcher

      Principal investigator of the projects
    • ORIGINS: Grounding Artificial Intelligence in the Origins of Human behavior (funding: Inria)
    • ECOCURL: Emergent communication through curiositydriven multi-agent reinforcement learning (funding: ANR)

  • 2017-2019

    Cogitai Inc., USA

    Research scientist

    • Cogitai, Inc. is dedicated to building artificial intelligences (AIs) that learn continually from interaction with the real world. Our goal is to build the brains, i.e., the continual-learning AI software, that will let everyday things that sense and act get smarter with experience. This experience will be shared across devices and domains to allow the rapid scaling-up of learning.

  • 2015-2017

    SPECS laboratory, UPF, Barcelona

    Post-doctoral researcher

    • Cognitive architectures for Human-Robot Interaction (WYSIWYD project)
    • Co-investigator of the INSOCO project (with Martì Sanchez-Fibla)
    • Emergence of social behavior in multi-agent populations ( SocSMCs project)
    • Modeling of synthetic consciousness (CDAC project)

  • 2012-2014

    Flowers lab, Inria, Bordeaux

    Researcher

    • Self-organisation of early vocal development
    • Explauto: a curiosity-driven learning framework
      • Open-source Python library

    LPPA, Collège de France -- CNRS, Paris

    Post-doctoral researcher, ROMEO project

    Decision making under uncertainty: a quasimetric approach

  • 2009

    Visiting scholar

  • 2007-2011

    • Sound system emergence in COSMO agent populations (target paper of a Special Issue: On the cognitive nature of speech sound systems)
